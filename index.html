cean Tours

Welcome to Ocean Tours

Ocean Tours is a renowned boat company in Iceland that offers a wide range of exciting trips including whale watching, diving, and island tours. The company has a reputation for providing unique and immersive experiences, which make it a popular choice for tourists looking to explore Iceland's breathtaking coastline and discover its incredible marine life.

Diving

Ocean Tours also offers diving trips for those who want to explore Iceland's underwater world. These diving trips provide guests with an opportunity to discover the diverse marine ecosystem of Iceland, from coral reefs to shipwrecks, and other fascinating sights.

Island Tours

Island tours are also available, where guests can explore Iceland's beautiful islands and their unique history. The tours give visitors a chance to discover Iceland's natural beauty and cultural heritage.

Other Tours

Apart from these tours, Ocean Tours also offers private boat charters, photography tours, and Northern Lights tours. Private boat charters allow visitors to have exclusive access to the boat, giving them the flexibility to customize their itinerary. The photography tours provide guests with an opportunity to capture Iceland's stunning scenery and wildlife in their cameras. The Northern Lights tours take visitors on a journey to witness the mesmerizing natural phenomenon of the Aurora Borealis.

Bird Watching Tours

Embark on a unique opportunity to spot rare and beautiful bird species in their natural habitats with Ocean Tours. Our bird watching tours take you to the coastal cliffs and other areas where birds gather to nest and feed, including the famous puffins. With expert guides to help you spot and identify different species, you'll have an unforgettable experience.

Northern Lights Tour

Experience the magic of the Northern Lights with Ocean Tours. Our Northern Lights tour takes you to remote and secluded locations with minimal light pollution, giving you the best chance of witnessing this incredible natural phenomenon. With expert guides to help you understand the science behind the lights and ensure you have the best viewing experience, this tour is perfect for anyone looking for a unique and unforgettable adventure.

Whale watctching

We are a company that offers whale watching tours in the beautiful waters of the Pacific Ocean. Our experienced guides will take you on a journey to see some of the most majestic creatures on earth.
